Frequently Asked Questions

Usability

Organizer

The Organizer is treated like any other participant as far as matching goes. The only difference between participants and the organizer is that the organizer has control over the optional price limit, and the format of the emails. Please keep in mind the organizer is a player too, and as such they will also be randomly matched with another participant.

Price Limit

The price limit is entirely optional. Some groups like to have a price limit so that all members spend roughly the same amount. If your group doesn't, simply leave the limit empty or with a '0'.

Email Template: Subject Line

The email template section allows the organizer to customize the email participants will receive. The subject line should let participants know why they are receiving the email, so they don't accidentally delete or ignore the email.

Email Template: Body

The email template section shows the organizer what the email will look like when it is received. If the organizer elects to set a price limit, the email will tell the participants what that price limit is. PARTICIPANT will be replaced with the name of the person receiving the email, and MATCH will be replaced by the name of the person the participant will be buying a gift for. Aside from setting a price limit, the email body cannot be changed by the organizer.

Participants

Please enter a valid name and email address for each participant. Press the red "Add Participant" button to add an additional participant. Press the red "Remove" button to remove a participant if you have added too many.

Import CSV File

Want to add participants more quickly? Its simple! Just upload a CSV file. To create a CSV file create a new text file, and on each line of the file write the participant's name, followed by a comma, and then their email address. Place each participant on a new line. When all participants have been entered, save the text document as a .csv file.

Other Questions

Does this website save any information?

No! After all emails have successfully been sent out, all of the data you entered into the website is purged from our servers. You will never receive another email from us (unless you use the site again next year!).
